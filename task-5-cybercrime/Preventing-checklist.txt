Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist


1. Never share OTP, PIN, or banking details with anyone.
2. Do not approve unknown or unexpected UPI payment requests.
3. Always verify the sender before making any online transaction.
4. Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts.
5. Enable two-factor authentication for extra security.
6. Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages.
7. Download apps only from official app stores (Play Store/App Store).
8. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history.
9. Use secure internet connections and avoid public Wi-Fi for payments.
10. Immediately report fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in).
11. Call helpline number 1930 in case of financial fraud.

## 📌 Project Overview
This Digital Literacy Project is designed to develop essential skills required to effectively and responsibly use digital technologies in today’s world. The project focuses on enhancing awareness about digital tools, online safety, professional communication, and cybercrime prevention.

Through a series of practical tasks, I explored different platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, Kaggle, HackerRank, and Google Forms. The project also emphasizes the importance of maintaining a professional online presence and following ethical practices while using the internet.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To understand the concept of digital literacy  
- To learn safe and responsible use of the internet  
- To build a professional digital portfolio  
- To explore coding and online tools  
- To gain awareness about cybercrime and prevention methods  

---

## 📂 Project Structure

### 🔹 Task 1: Infographic
Created an infographic using Canva to spread awareness about digital literacy, including safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and online professionalism.

---

### 🔹 Task 2: Digital Portfolio
Developed profiles on:
- GitHub (with profile README)  
- LinkedIn (professional profile)  
- Kaggle (data science platform)  

---

### 🔹 Task 3: Platforms Exploration
- Solved a basic coding problem (“Solve Me First”) on HackerRank  
- Created a Google Form titled *Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz*  
- Collected responses using Google Sheets  

---

### 🔹 Task 4: Email Etiquette
- Wrote formal emails (assignment extension & internship request)  
- Created a social media do’s and don’ts checklist  

---

### 🔹 Task 5: Cybercrime Awareness
- Studied a case of UPI fraud  
- Created a prevention checklist for safe online practices  

---
